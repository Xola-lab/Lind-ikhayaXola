PROJECTS

PREDICTING HOUSE PRICES WITH LINEAR REGRESSION

The primary objective of this project was to develop a predictive model that accurately estimates housing prices based on various features. By utilising multilinear regression, I aimed to create a robust model capable of providing valuable insight for both buyers and sellers in the housing market.

The data was obtained from Kaggle. The data was already clean so not much preprocessing was required. I selected relevant features to enhance the model’s performance. Leveraging multilinear regression, I trained the predictive model on the dataset to establish the relationship between the selected features and price.

WINE QUALITY PREDICTION

In this project, I leveraged my skills to predict wine quality based on fundamental chemical characteristics, focusing on Density and Acidity

The project began with thorough data exploration and preprocessing, ensuring data quality and relevance. I then implemented three classification models: Random Forest, Stochastic Gradient Descent, and Support Vector Classifier. Each model underwent rigorous training to optimise predictive performance.

Following model development, I conducted comprehensive model evaluation using established metrics to determine accuracy and precision. Through visualisation, I effectively communicated the comparative performance of each model, offering clear insights into their strengths and weaknesses.

EDA ON RETAIL SALES DATA

In this project, I embarked on an in-depth exploration of customer demographics and spending patterns.

Firstly, I checked if the data was to clean, to ensure data quality and the accuracy of my analysis. Next, I conducted demographic segmetation, examining factors such as age and gender to understand the data better. Then, I delved into spending behaviour, exploring purchase frequency, average trasaction value, and product preferences. Throught a combination of bar charts, histograms, and line charts, I visually depicted my findings, making complex patterns easy to grasp.

Ultimately, this project provided actionable insights for retail businesses, which could enable them to tailor marketing strategies and optimise product offerings to meet the diverse needs of their customer base.
