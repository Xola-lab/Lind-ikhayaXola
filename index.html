PROJECTS

PREDICTING HOUSE PRICES WITH LINEAR REGRESSION

The primary objective of this project was to develop a predictive model that accurately estimates housing prices based on various features. By utilising multilinear regression, I aimed to create a robust model capable of providing valuable insights for both buyers and sellers in the housing market.

The data was obtained from Kaggle. The data was already clean so not much preprocessing was required. I selected relevant features to enhance the model’s performance. Leveraging multilinear regression, I trained the predictive model on the dataset to establish the relationship between the selected features and price.

FROM DATA TO INSIGHTS: AN EXCEL ANALYSIS

In this project, I set out on a journey to transform raw Sales data into valuable insights using Microsoft Excel. I started by cleaning and structuring the dataset, ensuring accuracy and consistency with tools like Formatting and Pivot Tables -- allowing me to confidently move forward with the analysis.

Through descriptive statistics and visualizations, I was able to bring the data to life. I used charts and graphs to make sense of the numbers, revealing key insights and identifying anomalies that weren’t immediately apparent. This phase really highlighted my ability to interpret complex datasets and extract meaningful stories from them.

Finally, I compiled everything into a comprehensive report that tied my findings together. By using dynamic visualizations alongside a clear narrative, I was able to present my insights in a way that was both engaging and easy to understand. This project not only showcased my technical skills in Excel but also my ability to tell a compelling story through data—turning numbers into actionable insights for decision-makers.

EDA ON RETAIL SALES DATA

In this project, I embarked on an in-depth exploration of customer demographics and spending patterns.

Firstly, I checked if the data was clean, to ensure data quality and the accuracy of my analysis. Next, I conducted demographic segmentation, examining factors such as age and gender to understand the data better. Then, I delved into spending behaviour, exploring purchasing frequency, average trasaction value, and product preferences. Through a combination of bar charts, histograms, and line charts, I visually depicted my findings, making complex patterns easy to grasp.

Ultimately, this project can provide actionable insights for retail businesses, which could enable them to tailor marketing strategies and optimise product offerings to meet the diverse needs of their customer base.
