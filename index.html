PROJECTS

PREDICTING HOUSE PRICES WITH LINEAR REGRESSION

In this project, I aimed to develop a predictive model for estimating housing prices using Python and its Data Science libraries, applying multilinear regression for accurate predictions. The dataset, sourced from Kaggle, was preprocessed to ensure data integrity, followed by a selection of key features to optimise model accuracy and performance.

Using the regression model, I explored correlations between the chosen variables and housing prices, uncovering trends and patterns that provided deeper insights into the market. This stage emphasised my proficiency in handling complex datasets, performing feature engineering, and drawing data-driven conclusions.

The results were then presented through regression plots, supported by dynamic visualisations and statistical summaries. By clearly articulating the analysis, I was able to translate raw data into actionable insights, enhancing decision-making processes for potential buyers and sellers. This project demonstrated both my technical expertise in regression modeling and my ability to communicate data findings effectively.

EDA ON RETAIL SALES DATA

In this project, I embarked on an in-depth exploration of customer demographics and spending patterns, utilising Python for exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover trends and relationships within the data, providing a comprehensive understanding of consumer behaviour.

Firstly, I ensured the data was clean to maintain the quality of the dataset for accurate analysis. I then performed demographic segmentation, analysing variables like age and gender to extract key insights. I also analysed spending behaviour, focusing on purchase frequency, average transaction value, and product preferences. Using visualisations like bar charts, histograms, and line graphs, I represented the trends effectively, making complex patterns easy to interpret.

Ultimately, this project can provide actionable insights for retail businesses, which could enable them to tailor marketing strategies and optimise product offerings to meet the diverse needs of their customer base.
